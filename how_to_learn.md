# **How to Learn Effectively**

## 1. **Reasoning**

* Understand *why* you want to learn a specific topic.
* Define the personal or practical purpose behind your learning.
* A clear reason sustains motivation and guides focus.

---

## 2. **Embrace Jealousy**

* Be honest with yourself about your feelings.
* Jealousy often reveals what you truly desire to achieve.
* Instead of denial, use it as a compass to set meaningful goals.

---

## 3. **Design Your Syllabus**

* Break down your goal into **main topics** and **subtopics**.
* Use **open-source materials** or existing curricula as initial references.
* Refine and tailor your syllabus to match your learning objectives.

---

## 4. **Create Your Own Notes**

* Write notes that are **systematic, clear, and accessible**.
* Use tools like **GitHub**, **Notion**, **Paperpile**, **ResearchRabbit** and etc.
